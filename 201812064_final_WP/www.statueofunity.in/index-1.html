Important Notes

  • 1) Visitors to take a note that Statue of Unity Site Entry Opens at 09:00 AM and Closes at 05:00 PM.
  • 2) Payment Gateway Charges
    • A) No Charges for booking ticket via Net Banking or Debit Card.
    • B) 1% + GST on Total Amount will be charged for booking via Credit Card.
  • 3) In case your Transaction failed and amount deducted from your bank account.
    • A) Please mail failed Transaction screen short and transaction id on E mail,
           [email protected] or call on 9737078662 / 63.
  • 4) “SVPRET reserves the rights to cancel / refund your trip / booking”.
  • 5) To View Booked Tickets, Follow Below Steps.
    • A) Open https://soutickets.in/index.php
    • B) Click on “Login” (Top Right Corner of The Website).
    • C) On Login page, Enter the Mobil number from Witch you have Booked the tickets and Click "Send OTP".
    • D) On Receipt of One time Password, enter the OTP on Website, you will be Redirected to Home Page.
    • E) On the Top Right side of the Website, Visitor can See the registered name.
    • F) On Clicking name Three option will be visible.
    • G) From the visible three options Click “My Tickets”.
    • H) You Will be able to View your booking for SOU.
